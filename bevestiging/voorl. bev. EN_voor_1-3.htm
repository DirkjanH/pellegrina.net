Cancellation

If La Pellegrina is unable to offer a place, or is forced to cancel a course, any fees that have been paid will be entirely refunded. Deposits cannot be refunded to applicants who withdraw from the course. Applicants cancelling after 15 June are obliged to pay the entire course fee. It is therefore advisable to take out a cancellation insurance. La Pellegrina retains the right to change tutors and programmes if such changes are considered necessary.

Reduction fund

We are very grateful for having received a number of gifts and pledges for our reduction fund. This donation will facilitate offering reductions to music students and Eastern European participants, and thus help admitting people who are interested in the courses but unable to pay the full fee. Please take a look here the report about last year's received contributions and granted reductions. {donatie}
